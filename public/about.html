Our Mission

We exist to democratize space travel.

SharedStars equips individuals with:

  • Certifications to qualify for future space missions.
  • Immersive AR/VR experiences that simulate real astronaut training.
  • A 10-module space readiness program designed by industry experts.
  • Leaderboards and gamification to create a competitive but supportive training environment. A vibrant community of Subscribing Space Travelers (SSTs) shaping the future of space exploration.

With over 1.17 billion space enthusiasts worldwide, SharedStars bridges the gap between aspiration and accessibility, ensuring every dreamer has a path to space.

A SharedStars certification is your ticket to the fast-growing space travel industry, setting a benchmark similar to OSHA and ISO standards in aerospace and occupational health.

Our Story

From an Impossible
Dream to a Movement

At one of the lowest points in my life, I felt that my dream of space travel was impossible. The cost of launching into orbit-ranging from hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars-made it seem like a privilege reserved for the ultra-wealthy.

Tormented with yet another night of Netflix I stumbled upon a new Series, "Three Body Problem" and from that moment on my life slowly began to change. After the Series ended my quest for more Sci-fi information remained, so I read and read and read Liu Cixin's trilogy as well.

Then, I discovered The Three-Body Problem by Liu Cixin. The book's themes of human resilience, innovation, and the power of collective effort reignited my determination.

What if space travel didn't have to be reserved for the elite?

That moment of inspiration led to SharedStars.

This isn't just a training platform-it's a movement.

We're dedicated to making space travel a reality by providing the education, community, and opportunities needed for anyone to prepare for the next frontier.
